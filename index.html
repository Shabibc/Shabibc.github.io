About Me

I'm the kind of person who loves taking ideas and actually making them happen. I'm always curious, super creative, and I like solving problems in ways that actually make sense for people. Half of me loves going crazy over design details and strategy, while the other half isn't afraid to try something totally new.

What I Love Most

From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by how people connect whether through shared interests, creative collaboration, or everyday transactions. This curiosity, paired with my drive to solve problems, inspired me to create Fusion, an online marketplace. I love coding because it lets me build cool stuff from scratch like solving puzzles but way more fun. Design is my way of making things not just work, but look awesome too. When I mix both, I can create things that are easy to use and nice to look at, which is exactly what I did with Fusion. I’m the kind of person who gets excited over clean code and bold colors, and I think tech should be as creative as it is functional.

What is Fusion?

Fusion is an online marketplace designed to make buying and selling easy for everyone. Whether you're a small business, an artist, or just someone cleaning out their closet, our platform helps you connect with buyers simply and safely. We focus on keeping things user-friendly, affordable, and fair so that anyone no matter their experience can buy or sell with confidence. Fusion is all about bringing people together in one convenient place.
